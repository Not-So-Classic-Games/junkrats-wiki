## Character Roles

Characters within the *JunkRats* setting generally fall into several categories.

**Runners**  
Individuals sent from [[The Underground]] to recover resources and technology from [[surface|The Surface]].

**Underground Residents**  
Members of the crater settlement responsible for maintaining infrastructure, security, and daily operations.

**Surface Survivors**  
Individuals who live or operate outside the settlement in the ruins of the city.

**Independent / Unknown**  
Characters whose affiliations or loyalties are unclear.
