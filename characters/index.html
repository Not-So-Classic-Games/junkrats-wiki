    Characters Index

    The Characters section documents individuals who appear within the JunkRats setting.

    These pages describe notable survivors, leaders, and other figures connected to the world following The Collapse. Character articles typically include background information, affiliations, roles in the story, and relationships with other characters, locations, or factions.

    Main Characters

    These individuals form the primary group followed throughout the JunkRats narrative.

    Adrian

    Leader of the JunkRats and a former Runner from the Underground.

    Lucy

    Protector of the JunkRats and an experienced Runner.

    Gabriel

    Technical specialist responsible for operating and restoring abandoned systems.

    Zariah

    Weapons specialist who joins the group after surviving independently on the surface.

    Supporting Characters

    Supporting characters appear throughout the story or play roles in the broader world of the JunkRats setting.

    Vince

    Leader of the Underground and one of the architects of the crater settlement.

    Michael

    An individual connected to events surrounding the JunkRats.

    Character Roles

    Characters within the JunkRats setting generally fall into several categories.

    Runners
    Individuals sent from The Underground to recover resources and technology from The Surface.

    Underground Residents
    Members of the crater settlement responsible for maintaining infrastructure, security, and daily operations.

    Surface Survivors
    Individuals who live or operate outside the settlement in the ruins of the city.

    Independent / Unknown
    Characters whose affiliations or loyalties are unclear.
